How it works...

RSS-to-email has never been easier. Include multiple RSS feeds in your email campaigns including articles, podcasts and videos.

Boosting ROI & Engagement.

01

Automate content from multiple RSS feeds in your emails

We make it easy to include content from multiple RSS feeds in your email campaigns through our simple, code-free platform. These can represent categories or interest groups (such as Local News; Business News; Jobs; Sport; Fashion) or different channels (like Blog; Podcast; Videos).

02

Match your content to subscriber preferences

Each RSS Feed can be linked to an interest group, representing your subscribers' preferences. This ensures that the right content reaches the right people. You can also set any RSS feed to appear for everyone so all subscribers receive the content. You're in complete control.
